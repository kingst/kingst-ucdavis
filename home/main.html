Computer Science @ UC Davis

My research focus is on technology to manage Type 1 Diabetes. Managing Type 1 Diabetes is hard, and I believe that novel software, hardware, and AI can help make it easier.

I spent eight years in industry including time at two startups that I founded, Twitter, Lyft, and Stripe. I wrote a blog post about why I moved from academia to industry and back.

Fun fact: I'm the most hard-core biohacker at UC Davis, bar none. No, I don't microdose or take drugs and I barely even take supplements. Yet, it's not even close. I'm by far the most hard-core biohacker on campus.
